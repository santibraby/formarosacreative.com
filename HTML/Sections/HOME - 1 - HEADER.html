ABOUT

BIO

Forma Rosa Creative is a Brooklyn design atelier offering creative direction and design development for luxury brands, galleries, and hospitality designers.

We specialize in experiential design, spatial concepts, and custom furniture and lighting — orchestrating bespoke teams to realize projects at the intersection of computational design and artisanal craft.
